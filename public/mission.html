Current Mission: Coopy

We are excited to start raising money for our 11th mission, Coopy! Coopy was recently rescued by CrisisDogsNC (for more information visit https://www.crisisdogsnc.org/). Coopy is an extremely sweet mixed terrier whose owner was unable to afford to keep him. He has two fractures in his front leg and is in desperate need of surgery. Our goal is to raise $1500 to help CrisisDogsNC cover the costs of his medical bills.

Our mission is to help other animals like Coopy, whether that be with their medical or shelter needs. You can help us reach our goal and make a difference in Coopy's's life by purchasing our rings or donating directly to their cause. 100% of the profits made by our rings will go towards animals in need, currently focusing on Coopy!
